1 Introduction

Meo Pac ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Meo Pac, developed and operated by Sehat Poin.

By downloading, installing, or using Meo Pac, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information in order to provide and improve the Meo Pac experience. The types of information we collect are outlined below.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information such as your email address (if applicable)
  • User-generated content submitted within the app

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App usage data such as game sessions, features accessed, and preferences
  • Game progress, scores, and settings stored locally on your device

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain core functionality of Meo Pac
  • To store your game progress and preferences locally on your device
  • To improve, personalize, and optimize the app experience
  • To analyze app usage and performance for product improvement
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or unauthorized use
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

4 Data Storage

Local Storage Only

Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using shared preferences. This data remains on your device and is not automatically transmitted to our servers.

Meo Pac does not require user registration or cloud sync. All game data is retained locally on your device. Clearing the app data will remove all stored preferences.

5 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent — We may share information when you explicitly agree.
  • Legal Compliance — We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Service Providers — We may engage trusted third-party providers to assist in app operations, subject to confidentiality obligations.

6 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining appropriate safeguards.

7 Children's Privacy

Meo Pac is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
